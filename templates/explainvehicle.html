Your automobile rental company’s fleet can be rented out through our secure and reliable system ensuring safety for you and the guests.

What vehicles qualify for renting out?

Up to 3 year old registered vehicles in pristine condition, accompanied by a chauffeur to drive guests to their destinations.

Types of vehicles that can be shared

You can rent out anything from a coaster to a sedan and a pick up truck to a motorcycle. Any vehicle that fulfills the requirements of a guest is a great vehicle to rent out!.

Duration of rentals

Your automobile can be rented out anywhere from a day to a week and more! Guests can only make a reservation of vehicles you have made available to them at the time they’re available..
